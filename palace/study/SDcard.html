You're looking for something new

The year is 2025, and everything feels just a little unstable. Kimodameshi, a figure drawing event, is soon coming to a close, and you've been thinking about exploring new art mediums.

You've seen some fun projects online recently, and so you decide to buy a digicam from 2011. You commit to taking a photo a day for the entire next month. The climb begins.


Photo April

As mentioned in the introduction, I've decided to take at least a photo a day in April, sort of inspired by the plenairpril challenge. I've given myself some guidelines to follow:

  • The photo has to be interesting, according to my own judgement. This ensures that I put in an effort; Make art!

  • Photo has to be taken that day. No batching!

  • The photo has to be taken using manual control. No auto exposure, no AI enchance!

  • The photo can be anything. This includes photoshop jobs, mixed media, etc!
