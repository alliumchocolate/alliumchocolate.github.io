Another year goes by: 2023

I've improved significantly in 2023; It was a time of several major art breakthroughs for me, but also included my longest break from art since I started, for about 2 months between September and October.

One of my main 2023 projects was the first (and, as of writing, the only) written guide to the indie game Faerie: Afterlight. I got really into it lol, spending over 60 hours throughout december on the project.




Chroma Corps 2023
