Instructions

Want to make one of these papercrafts below? Great!

Step 1: Print

Any paper will do - even printer paper! For best results, I recommend something above 100g/m2 - generally, the higher the weight, the more sturdy the final papercraft will be. The pages are in the A4 format.

Step 2: Cut

Cut out the individual pieces along the solid thick lines, using scissors or a craft knife. Watch out for sharp tools!

Step 3: Fold

Every template comes with a key - use it to fold the paper along the lines. You can score the paper before hand, using a folder, or by (lightly) pressing the paper with a craft knife, to make this step easier!

Step 4: Glue

Using glue or tape, stick the paper together according to the tabs. The numbers are the recommended order - when you have the model in 3D, it should be pretty clear where each tab should glue into. Remember that you can use the photos and the template itself for reference!


And that's it! Enjoy building!
