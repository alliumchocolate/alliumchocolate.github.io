In a quiet, sunbathed corner of one of the many courts, you find an old greenhouse. A teal platina has covered all of the exposed copper, and the glass has become cloudy from age.

Walking inside, it seems oddly larger than it's exterior would allow; Plants are stacked row after row, interlaced with water works, careful arrangements of lamps and decorative stones.
