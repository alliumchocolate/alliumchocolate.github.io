Gate Greenhouse Palace grounds Attic Craftsroom

You've wandered the Palace for a while, exploring the sights, but this room has stopped you in your step. A simple, weathered attic, filled with dusty trinkets.

The quiet and humble nature of this place is a breath of fresh air in the grandness and scale of the surrounding towers.

You spot a light ray make its way through the roof, only to softly land on a covered pile of furniture. Pulling the cloth aside throws the settled dust into a dance and reveals a very plain music player.
