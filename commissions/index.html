What is a papercraft?

Also sometimes called a paper toy, a papercraft is a buildable toy made of paper!

In this context, this means a template that you can print, cut out and then glue together to construct a physical paper figure.


What can I expect?

Template

You will get a print-ready, buildable template like the one here of your chosen subject, be it a character, your pet, or really, any object üíõ

Process

Due to the low-poly nature of papercrafts, things work best in a cutsey style! I will work with you to develop the design into a fun and playful craft.

Format

You will get a 600 DPI file in .PDF (or .SVG, if you prefer) format.

How does a built papercraft look like?

As you can see below, constructed papercrafts have a geometric, low-poly appearance, similar to origami or old 3D games. Templates printed on a single A4 page make a model roughly the size of a fist. Click to zoom in!
