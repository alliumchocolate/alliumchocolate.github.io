The Pride Caf√© has started as a Minecraft Hypixel housing back in 2021, but since then, has grown into a small queer community.



Over the years, the project has slowed down, until eventually, mid-way through 2025, we've decided to put a soft-close on it's story.



This page serves as a tribute to the Pride Caf√© and the people who have shaped it.
