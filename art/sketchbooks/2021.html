Sketchbook 1

This sketchbook is short, but you can tell it's important. In some ways, this book is the grandest of them all.

The year is 2021

A younger Allium is preparing to move out and start university, while working a summer job. And one day, for no real reason, they start drawing - at first, simply with a pen on paper.

A month goes by and I buy a cheap non-screen tablet. My first piece of digital art is created on the evening of August 18th, 2021. The climb begins.
